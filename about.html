About Us

Our Mission

Greenify Boston is dedicated to creating a cleaner, greener, and more sustainable city through direct action, community engagement, and policy advocacy.

We plant trees, clean up public spaces, push for stronger environmental legislation, and hold corporations accountable for their impact on our planet.

By empowering volunteers and working alongside local leaders, we strive to make Boston a model for urban sustainability—one tree, one cleanup, and one policy at a time.
